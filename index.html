Controlling Appliances through Auto Detection

Ever walked out of your room and forgot to swich the lights and fans off? And walked back only to realise that a huge electricity bill is awaiting you? By using tech devloped by Auto Everything, appliances automatically gets off if no person is detected for a long period of time.

Notifications

Our tech can send Notifications about appliances getting turned on/off, sending regular updates about energy consumption or alerting user about some suspicious activity.

Data Statistics And Analysis
